About This Site

This website was created as my Drafting Advanced project. The project consisted of learning: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript from scratch. This site was created in Adobe Dreamweaver CC 2017. Most learning done was self-taught on websites such as Codecademy and various YouTube tutorials. I didn't really know what to put on this site, so I use it as an all-purpose site for myself. I really enjoyed creating this site and the real-world applications I'm able to gain from it. One of the things I really like about this site is the presentations I have uploaded onto the site. Gone are the days of struggling to log onto my Google account on a teachers computer to try and get my presentation working. Instead I just have to type in my website and it's ready to go. I'm really grateful to have been able to get this opportunity to be graded on something I've always wanted to learn.
